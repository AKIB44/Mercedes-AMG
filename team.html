Meet The Team

Who we are & What we do?

Mercedes-Benz, a brand of the Mercedes-Benz Group, has been involved in Formula One as both team owner and engine manufacturer for various periods since 1954. The Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team, which is based in Brackley, England,[1] and possesses a German licence,[2] is as of 2022 majority owned by the Mercedes-Benz Group with Toto Wolff having a significant shareholding.
